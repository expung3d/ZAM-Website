Better Civilians

Better Civilians is a pseudo-faction created by the EZM team. Adding randomized civilians of different types to increase AI diversity.

Better Civilians's Information

Better Civilians were created by Expung3d to randomize the civilian AI creation. Different types have been added for civilians:

  • Normal Civilians
  • Paramedics
  • Press Reporters
  • Police

These AI have random faces, clothing, facewear, voices, etc. Making the civilians encountered in missions feel less copy and pasted. Spamming the AI creation modules (CTRL + LMB) will generate a ton of randomized civilian AI. This can easily spawn an entire towns worth of civilians that you'll just have to position throughout the town.
If you'd like to add civilians to Better Civilians contact Expung3d in the Discord.
